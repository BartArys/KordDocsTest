getMessagesAround

Requests a flow of messages created around the Message with the messageId in the channel with the channelId.Unlike getMessagesAfter and getMessagesBefore, this flow can return a maximum of 100 messages. As such, the accepted range of limit is reduced to 1..100.Supplied messages will be equally distributed before and after the messageId. The remaining message for an odd limit is undefined and may appear on either side.The returned flow is lazily executed, any RequestException will be thrown on terminal operators instead.
open override fun getMessagesAround(messageId: Snowflake, channelId: Snowflake, limit: Int): Flow<Message>
